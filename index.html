Kwaai Community Engagement & Vision Survey

Introduction

Hello Kwaai Innovator!

Thank you for being a part of the Kwaai community. We're on an exciting mission to democratize AI and build a future where technology empowers individuals. To do this effectively, we need your valuable insights and perspectives.

This questionnaire will take approximately 25-30 minutes to complete. Your honest feedback will help us:

  • Understand your skills, interests, and motivations.
  • Identify challenges and opportunities within Kwaai that you see.
  • Improve our onboarding, engagement, and contribution processes for you.
  • Develop a fair and rewarding incentive system that benefits all members.
  • Shape the strategic direction of Kwaai and its initiatives, including Oak Venture Studio, with your input.

Your input is crucial. The more detailed your feedback and proposals, the more effectively we can build a thriving ecosystem together. The information you provide will directly inform our strategies for monetization, resource allocation, and ensuring value flows back to our members and the community. We appreciate you!
